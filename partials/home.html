Welcome to GogglePal

We Tell Your Mountain Story

Track, Compete, and Share.

Imagine you are skiing or snowboarding at your favorite resort. Maybe it’s Vail. Or Whistler. Or Tahoe. Or the local hill in your back yard. Whether it’s your 100th time on the mountain or your first, GogglePal will make sharing your experience with friends and family easier and way more fun.

From playing an all-mountain treasure hunt to challenging your friends to ski or ride the most vertical feet, GogglePal’s stripped down version of augmented reality helps you tell your own story in the data that matters most. Track your top speed, the vertical you’ve accumulated, and even the energy you’ve spent.

All of this data can be summarized, scrutinized, and compared.

Your Reality. Your Vision.

In three simple steps, our Heads Up Display works like this:

  1. MOUNT the small GogglePal adapter to the goggles you already own
  2. LEARN about your speed and vertical with a simple heads-up display in your lenses
  3. PLAY with your friends by comparing data like top speed and playing games all across the mountain using the GogglePal heads-up display and mobile app.

Imagine getting off the chairlift and looking down on the slopes in front of you. There is a whole mountain of data before your eyes and with the GogglePal, you can begin collecting it. The mountain is yours.
